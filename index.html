Safety and Security

IoT Systems

In the domain of the Internet of Things (IoT), we are witnessing a notable trend characterized by the proliferation of cost-effective connected devices. However, the adoption of energy-efficient protocols introduces potential vulnerabilities.

To address these challenges, ACIS-IoT couples unexpected events and detected threats with original systems through refinement techniques, enabling sophisticated attack mitigation schemes.
