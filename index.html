ACIS-IoT: Safety and Security Assurance for Critical IoT Systems

Summary: In the domain of the Internet of Things (IoT), we are witnessing a notable trend characterized by the proliferation of cost-effective connected devices tailored for specific domains. However, the adoption of energy-efficient protocols for low-cost communication introduces potential vulnerabilities that compromise the quality of service. Moreover, IoT applications must accommodate high scalability to meet diverse user feature requirements. Unfortunately, during system execution, unforeseen threats and errors may arise that were not accounted for during the modeling phase. In order to address these challenges, it becomes necessary to couple unexpected events and detected threats with original systems through refinement or composition techniques. This enables the construction of more sophisticated attack mitigation schemes that enhance both security and scalability in IoT applications.

The project is funded by the National Center for Scientific Research (CNRS).
